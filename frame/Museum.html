International Mountain Museum

Pokhara’s International Mountain Museum offers a fascinating look into Nepal’s mountaineering history, the culture of Himalayan communities, and the majestic peaks that define the region. Exhibits include climbing gear from famous expeditions, detailed accounts of Everest ascents, and profiles of legendary Sherpas and climbers. The museum also explores geology, flora, fauna, and the diverse ethnic groups living in the Himalayas. It’s a great place to visit before or after a trek, providing both inspiration and context for the landscapes and stories that surround Pokhara.
