Gosaikunda

Gosaikunda is a sacred alpine lake located in the Langtang National Park, about 4,380 meters above sea level, and is one of Nepalâ€™s most revered pilgrimage sites. Known for its spiritual significance, Gosaikunda attracts thousands of Hindu and Buddhist pilgrims each year, particularly during the festival of Janai Purnima. The lake is surrounded by stunning snow-capped peaks, creating a serene and mystical atmosphere. Aside from its religious importance, Gosaikunda is also a popular trekking destination, offering trekkers a challenging yet rewarding journey through lush forests, high-altitude meadows, and rugged mountain terrain. The trek to Gosaikunda is part of the Langtang Trek and offers breathtaking views of the surrounding Himalayas, including peaks like Langtang Lirung and Ganesh Himal. The tranquil beauty of the lake, combined with the spiritual energy of the region, makes Gosaikunda a deeply transformative destination for both adventurers and spiritual seekers.
