Phewa Lake

The heart and soul of Pokhara, Phewa Lake is a serene freshwater lake that mirrors the snow-capped peaks of the Annapurna range on clear days. Lined with colorful wooden boats and the vibrant Lakeside promenade, it's a perfect spot for leisurely boating, lakeside dining, or simply watching the sunset paint the water in warm hues. At the center of the lake lies the Tal Barahi Temple, accessible only by boat, adding a mystical charm to the tranquil waters. Whether you're seeking calm reflection or a romantic ride on a traditional doonga boat, Phewa Lake offers a peaceful yet iconic Pokhara experience.
