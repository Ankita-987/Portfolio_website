Gupteshwor Mahadev Cave

Located just across from Davis Falls, Gupteshwor Mahadev is one of the longest caves in Nepal and holds deep religious significance. The cave houses a sacred Shiva lingam and features winding paths, limestone formations, and natural underground beauty.
