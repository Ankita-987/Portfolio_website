About Me

Hello! I’m Ankita Pokharel, a passionate and detail-oriented web developer with a strong desire to learn and grow every day. I enjoy crafting websites that are not only visually appealing but also intuitive and accessible.

I am currently a BCA student studying at Edmark College, affiliated with Tribhuvan University (TU). Balancing academics and my passion for coding has helped me develop practical skills and a disciplined approach to work.

My journey into web development began as a hobby, but it quickly turned into a deep interest. I have developed a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I am continuously exploring new frameworks and tools to enhance my skills.

Outside of coding, I enjoy gaming and creative arts, which inspire my innovative approach to challenges and help me think outside the box. I value collaboration and am always eager to connect with like-minded individuals and teams.

Looking forward, my goal is to contribute to impactful projects, build meaningful digital experiences, and continue evolving as a developer and creator.
